    About this register

    This register contains a record of notified acquisitions and applications for notification waivers. For notified acquisitions, entries include details of any public consultation being undertaken.

    Notified acquisition entries are generally created within one business day of receiving the notification. The ACCC’s determination and reasons are also published within one business day after a decision is made.

    Notification waiver application entries are published after a notification waiver decision is made, usually within one business day. 

    Learn more about the merger control regime and notifying an acquisition.

    For questions about:

    • a notified acquisition, email mergers@accc.gov.au
    • a notification waiver application, email notificationwaivers@accc.gov.au.

    Include the title of the acquisition in the subject line.

    If you have a general question about merger reform or the merger control regime, email MergerReformInfo@accc.gov.au.
